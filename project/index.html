Welcome to Blanding's Retreat

Escape busy of city life and immerse yourself in the serene beauty of Blanding, Utah. We offer unique Native American hogans and rustic cabins for rent, perfect for adventure and relaxation.

Our cabins and hogans provide a one-of-a-kind lodging experience that combines comfort with cultural significance. Water source is an artesian well. Cabins have propane heater for chilly nights, also refrigerator and stove.
