Site Plan for Blanding's Retreat

Site Name

Blanding's Retreat

This name reflects the unique accommodations available for rent in Blanding, Utah, emphasizing the local heritage and natural beauty.

Optional domain availability: blandingsretreat.com

Site Purpose

The site serves as a platform for promoting unique Native American hogans and cabins available for rent. It will provide information about the accommodations, local attractions, and outdoor activities in the Blanding area.

Scenarios

  • What types of cabins and hogans are available for rent?
  • What outdoor activities can I enjoy during my stay?
  • How do I book a stay at Blanding's Retreat?

Color Schema

  • Crimson Red (#B22222): Used for the header and section headings.
  • Desert Sand (#EDC9AF): Used for hover effects on navigation links and buttons.
  • Canyon Gray (#A9A9A9): Used for the footer background.

Typography

  • Font Family: Roboto
  • Used for all body text and headings to ensure readability and a modern look.
