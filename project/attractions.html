Local Attractions

Discover the beautiful outdoor activities and natural wonders around Blanding. Explore these attractions that showcase the unique culture and breathtaking landscapes of the area.

Bears Ears National Monument

Experience the stunning landscapes and cultural significance of Bears Ears, where ancient traditions meet breathtaking scenery.

Natural Bridges National Monument

Visit the impressive natural stone bridges and explore the hiking trails in this beautiful national monument.

Monumental Backcountry Hiking

Discover the hidden trails and unique geological features of the Monument Valley backcountry.

Valley of the Gods Loop

Take a scenic drive through the majestic landscapes of the Valley of the Gods and enjoy the stunning rock formations.

Edge of the Cedars State Park Museum

Learn about the ancient Puebloan culture at the Edge of the Cedars Museum, featuring artifacts and exhibits.
